About Me

I'm a mathematician and algorithm developer, specializing in control theory, optimization, scheduling, and stochastic processes. After completing my Ph.D. in applied probability, I've participated in Deep Learning program by Razor Labs and Nvidia. Afterwards, I've joined Axon-Vision as an algorithm developer, leading the research, development, and implementation of a scheduling algorithm for a resource allocation problem.
